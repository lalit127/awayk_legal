Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

Awayk ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Awayk handles your data. Unlike traditional apps, Awayk is designed with an offline-first, privacy-by-design architecture.

1. 100% Local Data Processing (No Cloud Uploads)

To help you wake up, Awayk utilizes advanced on-device Machine Learning (AI) missions:

  • Camera & Image Verification (Object Hunt, Sky, Grass, Bed, Smile): All image recognition and face tracking are processed 100% locally in your device’s secure enclave/RAM. No photos or video frames are ever recorded, stored on your device, or transmitted over the internet to any external servers.
  • Voice & Microphone Verification (Intention, Voice): Speech-to-text conversion is processed 100% locally on-device. No audio recordings or transcripts are ever stored or uploaded.
  • Health & Sensor Data (Steps, Shake, Squats, Pushups): Accelerometer readings are processed immediately on-device to determine mission completion and are not uploaded.

2. Face, Body, and Machine Learning Data Collection & Usage

In accordance with App Store guidelines, we explicitly disclose how our ML-based missions handle sensitive data:

  • Face Data (Smile Mission): Awayk uses on-device face detection technology (via Google ML Kit) to detect the probability of a smile. We do not collect, record, store, or transmit any face data or images to our servers or any third parties. The data is processed entirely locally on your device and is discarded immediately after the real-time processing is complete.
  • Body & Pose Data (Squats, Pushups Missions): Awayk uses on-device pose detection to track body movement and count repetitions. We do not collect, record, store, or transmit any body/pose data or images to our servers or any third parties. The data is processed locally and discarded immediately.
  • Object Data (Object Hunt, Environment Verification): Awayk uses on-device object detection to verify items (e.g., Sky, Grass, Bed). No images or object data are stored or transmitted.

3. Zero Account Requirement

You do not need to create an account, register an email address, or log in to use Awayk. Your alarm configurations, consistency streak statistics, and mission preferences are stored locally in your device's secure storage.

4. Subscription & Purchase Data

All premium subscription purchases (Awayk Pro) are processed securely through Apple App Store (In-App Purchases) or Google Play Billing. Awayk does not collect or store billing info; we only receive anonymous transaction tokens from the platform operators.

5. Third-Party Services

Awayk may use system-native APIs (such as Google ML Kit and Apple Vision) to execute local machine learning models. These libraries run locally inside the application bundle and do not transmit data to external services.

6. Policy Updates

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy inside the app.
